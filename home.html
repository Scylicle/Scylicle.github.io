THE BEGINNING

About

I would find myself as a calm, collective person. I can get active in certain situation, but overall, I am, me. While I do have some hobbies such as being a agricultural salesman, or night rides or walks, a certain hobby that catches my feelings and emotions best is photography.

It began with me taking standard photos from trips or experiences I had in my life. It would come out simple and nice. As time went on, during 8th grade, I took my first photo that had my eye. It was a gas station that I took on my bus with a dark type of feeling and mood. I edited it so the lights reflected from the glass appeared as stars, and with that began my journey.

Today, I still put effort into my photos and take them depending on the mood that area gives me, and what I am feeling in general. Even though others say the photos are good, I want to break through my limits, and try to always improve on my skills of photography. It may as well still be a start as of now, but that's okay. I will always find new ways to improve and to achieve new limits for photography.

Night Rides

Night rides or night walks really sooth my feelings and help me calm down. Being in stressful situations and going on a walk or ride helps me calm down, as if I was walking or driving away from my problems. Now I still resolve those problems, but I at least try to get a breather before I dive in.

AGRICULTURAL SALESMAN

For 8 months, I took care of my pig name Chris. P. Bacon. he took me on one hell of a journey. The cool fall, the freezing winter, and the humid summer all were with him last year, and I took him to auction and he got sold. I am just summarizing what happened and not going to tell all the experiences I had with him, that'll be way too much, but it was fun trying out agriculure, and hopefully, I can return someday.
